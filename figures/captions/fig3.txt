Figure 3. Movement controls distort the decision-signal estimate.

(a) Three control-validity tests applied to six candidate movement controls (none, linear, ridge,
pca, expanded, crossfit) on synthetic data whose injected signals are drawn from each session's own
movement covariance. For each control, three grouped bars give the cross-validated choice decode AUC
under three injected conditions: no-signal, with no choice signal added (dotted grey); preserve, with
a movement-orthogonal choice signal of 0.24 SD per cell added (solid blue); and remove, with a
pure-movement signal of 0.24 SD per cell added (hatched vermillion). The dotted horizontal line is
chance (AUC 0.50) and the dashed blue line is the uncontrolled (none) preserve level. The blue star
marks the expanded preserve bar and the vermillion star marks the pca remove bar. n = 28 co-recorded
midbrain (MRN) sessions; bar height is the mean decode AUC over 14 synthetic repeats and error bars
are 95 percent Monte-Carlo confidence intervals across repeats.

(b) Movement-controlled choice decode AUC against injected per-cell effect size in SD units, one line
per control, with the injected signal set to the measured choice-to-movement correlation (rho = 0.49).
The dashed horizontal line is the 0.57 recoverability bar, the dotted horizontal line is chance, and
the dotted vertical line is the 0.24 SD threshold injection. Colours and markers identify the control
as in the legend; the linear control is drawn with a thicker line. n = 28 sessions; points are the
mean over 14 synthetic repeats and shaded bands are 95 percent Monte-Carlo confidence intervals.

(c) Number of choice-selective cells whose choice signal remains significant after movement control on
the real Brain-Wide Map data, for three controls (expanded, linear, pca), out of 301 choice-selective
cells pooled across regions. The dashed line marks the linear count. Bars are raw survivor counts
(per-cell choice-AUC permutation test, p < 0.05, 2000 permutations); counts have no error bars.

Colour coding: in (a) and (c) blue or green denotes the validated linear control context, vermillion
denotes the over-correcting expanded control, and orange denotes the under-removing pca control; grey
denotes the uncontrolled condition. Print width 180 mm (double column).
